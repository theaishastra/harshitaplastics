Our Journey & Mission

Our journey began in 2019, and by 2022 we had set up a cutting-edge manufacturing unit at Plastic Park, IDA Mankhal, Telangana. With an expansive 40,000 sq. ft. production facility, our infrastructure is designed to meet the highest standards of cleanliness and hygiene, particularly for food packaging needs. Our facility houses state of art machines that are capable of producing a wide range of co extruded multilayer films, stretch films which are critical components in flexible packaging solutions.

At Harshita Plastic Products Pvt. Ltd. our goal is simple: to deliver top quality multilayer films that meet the evolving needs of our customers. We are committed to innovation, sustainability, and providing tailored solutions that enhance the value of our clients' products. By leveraging our extensive industry experience we continuously work to improve packaging materials, optimize production and deliver results that exceed expectations.

Associate Partner

Vinayak Polymers Inc.

Authorised Consignment Stockist of
GAIL India Ltd & BCPL (Polymer Division).

Vinayak Polymers Inc. was established in 2007, by Mr. Rahul Aggarwal as the Managing Partner and supported by his brother Mr. Ankit Aggarwal, to provide polymer and service-based solutions that accelerate the growth of its customers. It was founded with the philosophy based on helping customers find solutions to their raw material requirements and a commitment to continuous improvement and service.

Since 2017, we are consignment stockist of GAIL (India) Ltd.(polymer division), and BCPL for the states of Telangana and Andhra Pradesh in South India.

Built on trust, the company Vinayak Polymers Inc. is fast emerging a pioneer in polymer distribution business. In an Industry that is highly competitive and challenging we have differentiated ourselves on the quality of pre- and post-sales service that we offer and in-depth knowledge of the polymer trade we have.
