STRETCH FILMS

A highly stretchable plastic film commonly made from polyethylene and it is wrapped around items. Also referred to as stretch wrap, it is manufactured using extrusion process.

Stretch film has a high degree of memory once stretched, it offers higher load and stretch capacity, allowing product loads to remain better secured. Higher tear resistance is an advantage when securing loads with sharp edges.

Machine stretch wrap is ideal for protecting items from elements like dust and moisture. It also offers superior protection for items that may be subject to vibration or shock during transportation. Stretch films also protect items from ultraviolet rays. Stretch films are easy to wrap and can increase worker productivity.

A flexible, clingy plastic film made from polyethylene, stretch wrap is used to tightly secure goods and is produced through the extrusion process.

MULTILAYER FILMS

Multilayer films are structures composed of two or more layers of different polymers that are combined to achieve specific performance characteristics that are not possible with single layer.

Multiple polymers such as LLDPE, HDPE, LDPE, Metallocene are extruded simultaneously through a single die, creating a multilayer structure. The layers can be combined through co-extrusion and the selection of materials is tailored to various desired applications.

These films are used in different packaging applications, offering enhanced barrier properties, mechanical strength, puncture resistance, printability and sealability.





Multilayer films are widely used for various packaging needs such as :

Seed, grain & fertilizer packaging
Pharmaceutical packaging
Food & beverages packaging
High strength shrink wrap
Water & liquid packaging
High strength stretch wrap for domestic & industrial packaging
Opaque & natural film for oil & milk packaging
